Full Brand Identity: The Phoenix Fitness Relaunch

Client: Phoenix Fitness | Service: Branding, Logo & Strategy | Date: 3rd October 2024

The Challenge

Phoenix Fitness needed to transition from an aggressive gym identity into a modern, inclusive wellness brand. Their new target market valued balance over intensity.

  • Old-fashioned logo & typography
  • Inconsistent social media tone
  • No clear brand mission

The Solution

We developed the “Rise” concept — a rebirth into strength and wellness. This included a gold/navy palette, a minimalist phoenix icon, and a community-driven tone of voice.

  • Brand pillars: Resilience, Empowerment, Community
  • Minimalist phoenix logo mark
  • Full 30-page brand guide
