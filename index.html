Meet the Founder

Olamide John Emiola — the visionary and creative force behind THE WEALTH QUEST NEST — is passionate about transforming raw ideas into iconic, purpose-driven brands. Rooted in faith, creativity, and innovation, he built this platform to help businesses and individuals express excellence through design, branding, printing, and digital experiences.

A devoted Christian, Olamide believes that every gift God gives is meant to serve others. This isn’t just a business — it’s a calling. His journey into branding, design, and digital transformation began with a desire to create more than visuals; he wanted to help people birth purpose-filled ideas, build excellent brands, and break limits. Through seasons of learning, unlearning, and trusting God at every uncertain step, he discovered that creativity backed by faith is unstoppable.

In 2021, Olamide made a deliberate choice to grow in both skill and spirit. He poured himself into learning, designing, printing, coding, and teaching. Each lesson became a part of the blueprint for The Wealth Quest Nest — a creative space where vision meets strategy, and passion meets professionalism.

Here, we don’t just design — we build legacies. We don’t just offer services — we empower dreams. From branding to web development, print to mentorship, everything is done with kingdom excellence. Olamide continues to learn, grow, and give God all the glory, knowing this is just the beginning.

When he’s not designing or managing projects, you’ll find him praying, teaching, or planning the next big move — all with a heart of service and a mind fixed on purpose.

“My mission is to blend creativity with integrity — building designs that speak truth, inspire purpose, and leave an unforgettable impact.”
